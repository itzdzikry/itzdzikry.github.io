Publication

Publication

  • Articles must follow the template provided in the “Submission” menu.
  • Every submitted paper will be reviewed by at least one peer-reviewer.
  • Double-blind review: authors and reviewers do not know each other's identities.
  • Reviewing process considers novelty, methodology, impact, conclusions, and references.
  • All accepted papers will be published in the seminar proceedings.
  • Selected papers have the opportunity to be published in affiliated journals.
  • Journal publication may have an APC (Article Processing Charge) paid by the author.
