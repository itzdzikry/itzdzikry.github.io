About The Conference

The Bicone International Conference invites academics and researchers to participate and share their knowledge and experience to enhance the understanding and advancement of issues in transformational education, technology, and society within their respective countries in the era of Society 5.0

Featured Speakers

The BICONE international conference features prominent speakers from several countries, experts in the fields of education, technology, and social sciences who will provide valuable perspectives on empowering society through innovation, digital transformation, and human development. Through this conference, BICONE aims to explore new strategies and practical solutions to address global challenges and drive positive social change.
