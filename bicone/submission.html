Submission Procedure and Policies

GENERAL INFORMATION
  1. Manuscripts must be original, unpublished, and not under review elsewhere.
  2. Research topics must align with ethical standards.
  3. Priority is given to manuscripts from international collaborations.
  4. Each submitted paper will be peer-reviewed.
  5. Double-blind review method is applied.
  6. Reviewing criteria include novelty, method, impact, and references.
